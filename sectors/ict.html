Research Pillar 01

Sustainable ICT & Quantum Computing

Leading the technical standardisation of quantum-resistant architectures and energy-efficient digital infrastructures.

Technical Objectives

Standardising the Quantum Future

As we transition toward Quantum Computing, the need for robust, trustworthy standards becomes critical. Our research focuses on the intersection of **Quantum Key Distribution (QKD)** and sustainable data management.

Quantum-Resistant Cryptography

Developing benchmarks for cryptographic protocols that can withstand quantum-level processing while maintaining energy efficiency.

Sustainable Data Architectures

Reducing the carbon footprint of massive data centers through hardware-level standardisation and AI-driven cooling optimisations.

Standardisation Impact

  • ISO/IEC JTC 1/SC 42
    Active participation in Artificial Intelligence standards.
  • ISO/IEC JTC 1/WG 14
    Defining international benchmarks for Quantum Computing.
  • CEN/CLC JTC 13
    Cybersecurity and Data Protection at the European level.
