Research Pillar 02

Aerospace & Satellite Swarms

Advancing technical standardisation for autonomous space systems and orbital climate monitoring under the Sustainability Umbrella.

Technical Objectives

Intelligence in Orbit

Modern space missions rely on coordinated swarms of small satellites. Our research ensures these swarms can operate autonomously while adhering to global safety and debris-mitigation standards.

Autonomous Swarm Coordination

Developing standard protocols for Machine Learning models that enable satellites to perform collaborative maneuvers without ground intervention.

Earth Observation & Sustainability

Standardising the processing of high-resolution climate data to monitor environmental changes from orbit with maximum accuracy.

Standardisation Impact

  • ISO/TC 20/SC 14
    Technical standardisation for Space systems and operations.
  • CEN/CLC/JTC 5
    European coordination for Space standardisation.
  • Debris Mitigation
    Defining international norms for sustainable orbital end-of-life.
