Research Pillar 03

Construction & BIM

Technical standardisation for Digital Twins and Circular Economy workflows under the Sustainability Umbrella.

Technical Objectives

Digitalizing the Built Environment

The construction sector is undergoing a massive digital and green transformation. Our research provides the scientific validation needed to standardise Digital Twins and ensure the Circular Economy becomes a reality for Luxembourg's building industry.

BIM & Digital Twins

Standardising the exchange of data between Building Information Models (BIM) and real-time sensor networks to create accurate, energy-efficient Digital Twins.

Circular Economy Frameworks

Developing data requirements for "Material Passports" to enable the reuse and recycling of construction components in accordance with EU Green Deal standards.

Standardisation Impact

  • ISO/TC 59/SC 13
    Organization and digitization of information about buildings (BIM).
  • CEN/TC 442
    European committee for Building Information Modelling standardisation.
  • ISO/TC 323
    International standards for the Circular Economy.
