A Strategic Partnership

Bridging the gap between scientific excellence and international technical standardization since 2017.

Our Philosophy

Scientific Excellence for National Quality

The ILNAS-SnT research programme is a high-level collaboration between ILNAS and the University of Luxembourg's SnT.

By integrating PhD researchers into global technical committees, we ensure Luxembourg’s digital transition is built on internationally recognized standards.

Key Objectives

  • National Strategy: Implementing the Luxembourgish Standardization Strategy.
  • Skill Development: Training the next generation of Standardization Experts.
  • Visibility: Positioning Luxembourg as a lead technical contributor in ISO/CEN.

A Synergy of Experts

The ILNAS-SnT Research Programme is powered by a multidisciplinary team of international researchers and standardization experts. Our strength lies in the dual-leadership model:

Scientific Excellence Our SnT researchers provide the technical depth and scientific validation required to push the boundaries of ICT, Space, and Construction technologies.
Strategic Standardisation Our ILNAS officers translate this research into global influence, ensuring Luxembourg’s interests are represented in international technical committees (ISO/IEC/CEN).
