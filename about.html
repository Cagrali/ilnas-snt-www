The Programme

What is the ILNAS-SnT Synergy?

Launched in 2017 and now in its third consecutive phase (2025-2028), this partnership creates a unique bridge between the Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust (SnT) and the Luxembourgish Institute of Standardisation, Accreditation, Safety and Quality of Products and Services (ILNAS).

The programme integrates PhD researchers directly into international technical committees (ISO/IEC/CEN/CENELEC). This ensures that scientific breakthroughs aren't just published in journals, but are used to shape the technical standards that govern our global digital economy.
