The Programme

What is the ILNAS-SnT Synergy?

Launched in 2017 and now in its third consecutive phase (2025-2028), this partnership creates a unique bridge between the Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust (SnT) and the Luxembourgish Institute of Standardisation, Accreditation, Safety and Quality of Products and Services (ILNAS).

The programme integrates PhD researchers directly into international technical committees (ISO/IEC/CEN/CENELEC). This ensures that scientific breakthroughs aren't just published in journals, but are used to shape the technical standards that govern our global digital economy.

Why Standardization?

Standardization is the "silent engine" of innovation. It ensures:

  • ✔ Interoperability: Ensuring different technologies work together seamlessly.
  • ✔ Market Access: Helping Luxembourgish companies enter global markets through compliant products.
  • ✔ Sovereignty: Allowing Luxembourg to have a voice in the rules that govern ICT, Space, and Construction.

2025-2028 Strategic Focus

The Sustainability Umbrella

In its third phase, the programme aligns three critical pillars—ICT, Aerospace, and Construction—under a unified sustainability framework. This approach ensures that technical standards support both the digital transition and the ecological goals of the European Green Deal.
