ILNAS/SnT - 2025-2028 Research Program Kick-off

Marking a milestone in a decade-long partnership, the launch of the third consecutive programme, this new phase focuses on a cross-sectoral Sustainability Umbrella. The partnership will drive innovation and technical standardisation across three key strategic pillars: Information and Communication Technology (ICT), Aerospace, and Construction. By aligning these sectors under a unified sustainability framework, the programme aims to develop resilient, future-proof standards that support Luxembourg’s digital and ecological transition.
